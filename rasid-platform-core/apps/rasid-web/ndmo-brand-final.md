## Slide Layout Patterns
- Dark navy sidebar on right (~25% width) with white text navigation
- Main content area on left (~75%) with white/light background
- Orange accent for active/selected items
- Rounded rectangles for content cards
- Groups for icons (bcgIcons_Simulation, Shield, Efficiency, OnlineSurvey)
- Title slides: full dark navy background with light text
- Content slides: white bg with navy sidebar
